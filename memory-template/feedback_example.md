**The format that works best:**

Each rule has three parts:
1. **The rule itself** — what to do or not do
2. **Why** — the reason (so the AI can judge edge cases)
3. **How to apply** — when/where the rule kicks in

**Example rule:**

> **Rule:** Always confirm before making irreversible changes (deleting files, force-pushing to main, dropping production data).
>
> **Why:** I move fast and I trust AI suggestions too easily. A one-second confirmation prompt saves me from "oh no" moments that take hours to recover from.
>
> **How to apply:** for any action that can't be cleanly reverted, pause and ask explicitly. For reversible local edits, just do it.
